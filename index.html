Rock, Paper and Scissors

Introduction about the game

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a classic hand game that is simple, fun, and widely played worldwide. It's often used to make quick decisions or as a lighthearted competition between two players. The game has three possible moves:

  1. Rock: Beats Scissors but loses to Paper.
  2. Paper: Beats Rock but loses to Scissors.
  3. Scissors: Beats Paper but loses to Rock.

Players choose their moves simultaneously, and the winner is determined based on the rules above. If both players choose the same move, the game results in a tie. This game is easy to learn and relies on quick thinking and a bit of strategy!
